Developer & Civic Leader

From the Field to the Community

Chinedum Ndukwe spent five years in the NFL before turning his competitive drive toward a challenge with higher stakes: the affordable housing crisis. As founder of Kingsley and Company, he leads a Cincinnati-based development firm that pairs financial discipline with genuine community accountability.

Ndukwe's dual degree in Business Management and Psychology from Notre Dame—followed by executive programs at Harvard Business School and Wharton—gave him a framework for evaluating deals without losing sight of people. His projects, including The Blair and Victory Vistas, are built around the belief that quality housing is a platform for long-term opportunity.

Beyond the development work, Chinedum is a licensed real estate agent who has served on the Mayor of Cincinnati's task force for Immigration, the Notre Dame Athletics Monogram Board, and the Mercy Health Board of Directors. His civic footprint runs as deep as his development portfolio.
